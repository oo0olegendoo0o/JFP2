⚖️ The Compliance-First Framework

The primary concern for any established firm adopting AI is compliance (ASIC Report 798, Best Interests Duty, Privacy Act). This blueprint relies on one non-negotiable principle:

The Golden Rule

"AI Assists. Humans Advise. No system makes a recommendation on behalf of a client without explicit adviser review and approval."

By keeping AI as a "Co-Pilot" rather than an "Autopilot," you retain the 30 years of fiduciary trust you've built, while stripping away the administrative drag.

🛠️ The Recommended Tech Stack

You don't need a single, massive enterprise software replacement. You need targeted integrations that connect your existing systems.

📗

Xplan (Core Database)

Keep Xplan as your single source of truth. We just need to optimise the data fields so AI can pull from it cleanly without manual re-entry.

🎤

PlannerPal (Audio Intelligence)

Stop taking manual meeting notes. PlannerPal sits in the meeting, transcribes the audio, extracts client goals, and logs compliance notes directly to Xplan.

🤖

Use financial-services-specific AI to merge the meeting transcript with the Xplan data to generate a compliant, fully-formatted SOA draft in 15 minutes.
